About Me

Hi, I’m Samuel. I’m a software engineer, educator, and entrepreneur who believes that everyone has a unique purpose given by God. I’m passionate about helping people discover their gifts and apply them to create value for themselves and others.

Through Young Devs Ltd, the company I founded, I work with youth, businesses, and organizations to leverage technology for learning, growth, and solving real-world problems. My vision is to build a community of purpose-driven innovators who approach technology not just as a tool, but as a way to impact lives positively.

Faith, integrity, and service guide everything I do. I believe true success comes when your work aligns with God’s purpose, uplifts others, and inspires change.

What I Believe In

  • Faith First: I start every decision and project by seeking God’s guidance. True wisdom comes from aligning life with divine purpose.
  • Purpose Over Position: Titles or status don’t define success. Living your God-given purpose does.
  • Servant Leadership: I lead by serving and lifting others, fostering growth, humility, and collaboration.
  • Integrity & Excellence: I commit to doing my work with honesty, quality, and excellence in all things.
  • Empowering Others: I strive to create opportunities for people to grow, learn, and use their gifts for meaningful impact.
